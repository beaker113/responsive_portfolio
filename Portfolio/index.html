About me

I was born outside of Atlanta, Georgia and attended Auburn University with a major in hotel and restaurant management. After eight successful years in the service industry working positions from hotels and restaurants to catering services, I have found myself looking to enter the tech industry. In order to do this, I have completed the coding bootcamp at the University of California, Irvine and earned a certificate in full stack web development.

I was a member of Delta Kappa Epsilon fraternity at Auburn university, often excelled in my classes, and often succeded in working as a team in various intramural sports.
